My personal interests

I have a cat that I dedicate time to every day. A little more than that when he's sick. He's fond of taking walks outside on a leash (less to protect the birds and small animals than it is to save him from traffic). We meander around, and he chases just about any insect we happen upon.
Except wasps. He's finally learned to leave them alone.

As a computer science major, I feel obligated to say that I like computers. When they work, anyway. About eight years ago I built my first, and I've been slowly changing the parts out to keep it from getting too old. I won't claim to be an expert, but it turns out you don't need to be for people to ask you to put them together. None of them have caught fire yet.

Other than that, I've been dabbling in game development. I don't know if it will go anywhere. I've found it to be rewarding when it goes right and disappointing when it goes wrong. The amount to do is daunting if I think too much about it, and like any good developer I keep adding more features so the project can go on forever.
